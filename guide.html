Step-by-step instructions to create your first Telegram bot

Step 1

Open Telegram & Find BotFather

BotFather is Telegram's official bot that helps you create and manage bots.

  • Open your Telegram app (mobile or desktop)
  • In the search bar, type @BotFather
  • Click on the verified BotFather account (it has a blue checkmark)
  • Click "Start" to begin chatting with BotFather
Step 2

Create a New Bot

Use the /newbot command to create your bot:

  • Type /newbot and send the message
  • BotFather will ask you to choose a name for your bot
  • Enter any name you like (e.g., "My Awesome Bot")
  • This is the display name users will see
Step 3

Choose a Username

Next, you need to choose a unique username for your bot:

  • The username must end with "bot" (e.g., myawesomebot or my_awesome_bot)
  • It must be unique - if taken, try a different name
  • Only letters, numbers, and underscores are allowed
  • Usernames are case-insensitive

üí° Tip: If your desired username is taken, try adding numbers or underscores.

Step 4

Copy Your Bot Token

After creating your bot, BotFather will send you a token:

  • The token looks like: 123456789:ABCdefGHIjklMNOpqrsTUVwxyz
  • This token is the password for your bot - keep it secret!
  • Click on the token to select it
  • Copy it to your clipboard

Step 5

Activate Your Bot

Now use our platform to activate your bot:

  • Go back to the home page
  • Paste your bot token in the input field
  • Click the "Create Bot" button
  • Wait for the success message

‚úÖ Success: Your bot is now active and ready to use!

Step 6

Test Your Bot

Let's make sure your bot is working:

  • Open Telegram and search for your bot's username
  • Click "Start" to begin chatting with your bot
  • Your bot will automatically add reactions to messages!
  • Try sending different messages to see the reactions
